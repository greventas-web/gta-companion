---

## Buttons

Style:
- Rounded
- Premium

---

## Animations

- Fade In
- Slow Background Motion
- Smooth Hover Effects
- No distracting animations

---

## Performance Rules

- Fast loading
- Mobile first
- Smooth scrolling
- 60 FPS animations whenever possible

---

## User Experience

Every screen should answer:

1. What can I do here?
2. Where do I go next?
3. Can I do it in one click?
